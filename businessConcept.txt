### Subscription-Based Travel & Outdoor Activities Platform

Your idea is a **subscription-based app** that allows users to book outdoor activities and travel packages using credits included in their selected subscription plan.

#### How It Works for Users:

* Users subscribe to a plan created and managed by the app admin.
* Each subscription plan provides a specific number of credits.
* Users can use these credits to partially pay for travel packages or outdoor activities.
* Credits are **not fully redeemable per booking** — they only cover a portion of the total cost.
* The redemption limit per booking is determined by the travel partner.

#### How It Works for Travel Partners:

* Travel partners join the platform after admin approval.
* Based on the subscription plans available, partners define:

  * The maximum credit redemption allowed per booking.
  * Booking limits per plan.
* This gives partners control over how much discount/credit usage they allow.

#### Admin Role:

* Creates and manages all subscription plans.
* Approves and manages travel partners.
* Approves partner listings before they go live.
* Controls the overall ecosystem and plan structure.

---

### Core Value Proposition:

* **For Users:** Affordable access to premium travel and outdoor experiences through subscription-based credits.
* **For Partners:** Controlled discount exposure with predictable booking volume.
* **For Admin:** Centralized control over plans, partnerships, and marketplace quality.
